5.When will you get ArrayIndexOutOfBoundsException?
   ArrayIndexOutOfBoundsException occurs when we access an array, or a Collection, 
that is backed by an array with an invalid index. This means that the index 
is either less than zero or greater than or equal to the size of the array.
